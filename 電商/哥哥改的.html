Cookies

Hahow uses Cookies on our website. Cookies are small text files that are stored in the memory of your terminal via your browser or mobile device. They store certain information which your browser may (depending on the lifespan of the cookie) retransmit to us upon your next visit to our website.

There are different functions of the cookies:

  1. Strictly Necessary cookies: Those are cookies enable you to move around Hahow and use our features. Without these cookies, we are unable to provide certain features.
  2. Security cookies: Those are cookies we used to authenticate users, prevent fraudulent use of login credentials.
  3. Function cookies: Those are cookies allow our website to remember your preference, your language, the type of your mobile device and other information to provide the best user experience.
  4. Analytics cookies: Those are cookies we used to understand how you engage with our website, how we can improve our website to provide better service.
  5. Advertising cookies: Those are cookies we used to provide accurate marketing, for example, based on your previous interactions with Hahow and provide you most relevant classes information for your reference.
